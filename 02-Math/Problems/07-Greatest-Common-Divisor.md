# Problem 07 - Greatest Common Divisor (GCD)

## Problem Statement

Given two positive integers `a` and `b`, find their Greatest Common Divisor (GCD).

The GCD of two numbers is the largest positive integer that divides both numbers without leaving any remainder.

---

# Examples

## Example 1

Input

```text
a = 12
b = 18
```

Output

```text
6
```

Explanation

```text
Divisors of 12

1 2 3 4 6 12

Divisors of 18

1 2 3 6 9 18

Greatest Common Divisor = 6
```

---

## Example 2

Input

```text
a = 20
b = 30
```

Output

```text
10
```

---

## Example 3

Input

```text
a = 17
b = 13
```

Output

```text

```

---

# Constraints

- Both numbers are positive integers.

---

# Expected Output

Return the Greatest Common Divisor.
