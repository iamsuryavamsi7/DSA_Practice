# Problem 08 - Least Common Multiple (LCM)

## Problem Statement

Given two positive integers `a` and `b`, find their Least Common Multiple (LCM).

The LCM of two numbers is the smallest positive integer that is divisible by both numbers.

---

# Examples

## Example 1

Input

```text
a = 12
b = 18
```

Output

```text
36
```

Explanation

```text
Multiples of 12

12 24 36 48 ...

Multiples of 18

18 36 54 ...

Smallest common multiple = 36
```

---

## Example 2

Input

```text
a = 4
b = 6
```

Output

```text
12
```

---

## Example 3

Input

```text
a = 5
b = 7
```

Output

```text
35
```

---

# Constraints

- Both numbers are positive integers.

---

# Expected Output

Return the Least Common Multiple (LCM).
