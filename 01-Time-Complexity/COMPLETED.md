# Final Learning

Before solving any DSA problem, always ask:

1. What is the brute-force solution?
2. Can it be optimized?
3. What is the Time Complexity?
4. What is the Space Complexity?
5. Can I improve either of them?

These five questions form the foundation of problem-solving in Data Structures and Algorithms.
